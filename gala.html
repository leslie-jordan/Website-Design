We are a global community conservation organization that advances the vision and work of Dr. Jane Goodall. By protecting chimpanzees and inspiring people to conserve the natural world we all share, we improve the lives of people, animals and the environment.

The Gala

The Gala is how the Jane Goodall Institute raises funds to support our mission. It is guaranteed that 100% of your donations go towards helping Chimpanzees. The Gala will occur on February 12th, 2024 at 5pm.


The dress code is black tie.


The Gala will begin with a speech about our mission and why the Chimpanzee population needs our help.

At 6 o'clock, dinner will be served. The dinner will offer a wide variety of vegetarian options.

After dinner, attendees can then visit booths and discover more about our mission.


Our Goal Is 500,000 dollars.
